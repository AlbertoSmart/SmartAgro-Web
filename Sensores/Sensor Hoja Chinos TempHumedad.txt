1 Overview 
The leaf temperature and humidity sensor is a composite sensor, which can accurately 
measure leaf temperature and humidity at the same time. 
The temperature sensor is composed of high -precision NTC thermistor and transmitter. The 
transmitter has the functions of offset compensation and temperature calibration, which can 
accurately measure the temperature in the whole measuring range. 
The humidity sensor is based on the principle of dielectric constant measurement, and 
simulates the leaf features by imitating the shape of blades. It can accurately measure the 
amount of water or ice by changing the dielectric constant of leaf surface. The sensitivity is 
good, and trace moisture or ice crystal residues on the leaf surface can be detected.  
The leaf surface temperature and humidity sensor adopts waterproof design as a whole, 
which has low power consumption and can be monitored continuously for a long time. It is 
easy to install, and can be hung on the greenhouse or installed on the mast of the weather 
station. 
 
2 Characteristic 
1. Imitating leaf surface characteristics, temperature and humidity measurement is fast and 
accurate. 
2. It has high precision temperature correction function and can output temperature signal 
independently. 
3. The humidity measurement is sensitive and can accurately detect the moisture or ice 
crystal residue on the leaf surface. 
4.  Analog voltage/current can be selected, and RS485 signal can be output. 
5. It is encapsulated with ABS and epoxy resin, which is waterproof and moisture -proof and 
has a longer service life. 
 
3 Scope of application 
This product can be widely used in environment, greenhouse, laboratory, breeding, 
warehousing, construction, high -grade buildings, industrial plants and other environmental 
temperature and humidity measurement of plant surface.

8 Matters needing attention in use 
1. When you receive the product, please check whether the package is in good condition, and 
check whether the sensor model and specification are consistent with the product you 
purchased. 
2. The installation place should be far away from chemical corrosion environment.  
3. Sensors and wires should be far away from high-voltage electricity and heat sources. 
4. Avoid scratching of the instrument, maintain the integrity of the external protective film, and 
prolong the service life of the instrument. 
5. Rough treatment of the instrument will destroy the internal circuit board and precise 
structure.
