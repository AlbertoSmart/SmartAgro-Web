1.Product Introduce 
The soil oxygen sensor is applying electrochemical principles suitable for the study of monitoring soil 
oxygen content respiration and permeability, and the relationship between soil oxygen content and 
osmosis and the activity of soil microorganisms. It has the advantages of reliable performance, long 
service life and fast response speed. The soil oxygen sensor is a rugged and reliable oxygen 
concentration measurement device. Its electronic components are completely encapsulated in epoxy 
resin, and a filter at the front end prevents the soil particles from damaging the sensor, and can be easily 
buried in the soil for measurement. The measured data can reflect the change of the oxygen content in 
the soil in real time. 
2. Product Features

as breathing and breathability 
2. All-electronic package with Nylon shell. 
3. Signal output is stable.

5. Measurement methods 
If you are measuring on a hard surface, you should drill a hole first (the hole diameter should be bigger 
than the sensor probes), then insert it into the soil and compact the soil before measuring; the sensor 
should be protected from severe vibration and impact, let alone knocked with hard objects. Because the 
sensor is a black package, the sensor will heat up sharply (up to 50℃) under strong sunlight. In order to 
prevent excessive temperature from affecting the temperature measurement of the sensor, please pay 
attention to shading and protection when using it in the field or in the field.  
Please note the soil oxygen sensor can be moisture-proof but cannot be water-proof, so need 
burry in the soil at least 10-20cm deep(according to the site condition)  to avoid immersion of 
the sensor probe in water. 
1. Buried measure method 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
➢ Make a soil profile in the vertical direction, slightly deeper than the installation depth of the 
bottommost sensor, between 20cm and 50cm in diameter 
➢ Insert the sensor horizontally into the soil profile 
➢ After the installation is completed, the excavated soil is backfilled in order, layered and compacted, 
and horizontal installation is guaranteed. 
➢ If you have the conditions, you can put the removed soil in a bag and number it to keep the soil 
moisture unchanged, and backfill it in reverse order.

7. Precautions 
 
1. Please check whether the packaging is intact, and check whether the sensor model and specifications 
are consistent with the products you have purchased; 
2. Do not wire with power, and only after the wire is connected and checked for error, can the power be 
turned on; 
3. The user should not change the components and wires that have been welded before leaving the 
factory when the product is in use; 
4. The sensor is a precision device, please do not disassemble it by yourself when using it, let alone touch 
the diaphragm, so as not to cause damage to the product; 
5. Avoid sticky particles from entering the sensor, prevent moisture, so as not to affect the measurement 
performance.
